About

Professional with over 20 years of experience in Projection, Interactive Video Installations and Live Video Performances. Veteran projectionist who combined a love of programming and film to create environments where real time video processing is central. Frequently collaborates with other artists tackling new technical challenges by creating dedicated systems and software suites to fulfill the specific needs of each project.

Interactive Installations

Oscillatory Motion - 2021

Interactive installation by Beatrijs Albers and Reggy Timmermans

Design of three robots' structures, drive trains and various other components using OpenSCAD / Structural parts machining on a CNC using Fusion360 at FabLAB iMAL / Drive train and other parts design and 3d printing / E-bike like lithium battery pack custom design using 18650 spot-welded and a BMS / Motor control and sensor data collection using two Arduinos on each robot / Sensors' data interpretation path planning and sound playback in MaxMSP runing on a miniPC on each robot

Blogpost

Locusts / paperRain - 2021

Interactive installation in collaboration with George Van Dam and "Various Artists"

Dedicated Audio-Video player developed in MaxMSP / Interpretation of the output of 8 music boxes via piezzos microphones / Interactive playback of an "explosive video sequence" and a quadraphonic soundtrack

Blogpost

Inland Wonderland - 2021

Interactive sculpture by Alexandra Dementieva

Dedicated software developed in Godot game engine / Accelerometer data collection with an Arduino / Interactive 2d animation loop for the two screens within the sculpture using the sensor data as input to influence the scenario

Live Performances

Save the Last Trance for Me - 2018

Audio visual performance under the direction of Matthaei & Konsorten

Dedicated video player in MaxMSP/Jitter and Ableton LIVE featuring:
Editable cues for playback triggers & smooth transitions / Editable video effect chains using pixel shaders inside MaxForLive modules / Kinect tracking a lamp torch and mapping of its position to the screens to reveal or hide live video clips

Vimeo

UBIQUE - 2016

Ciné-Concert with the band Ethnophonics under the direction of Singkhéo Panya

Dedicated multichannel video player in MaxMSP/Jitter and Ableton LIVE / Editable cues for playback triggers & smooth transitions / Editable video effect chains using pixel shaders inside MaxForLive modules / Fast video sampling in MaxForLive for playback from a percussive MIDI controller

Youtube

Infinite Journey/ In This Together… - 2015

Dance performance by Eveline Van Bauwel

Software developed in OpenFrameworks to create interactive color compositions and realtime effects that are the sole scenic element on stage / The colors pallets used are gathered in real time by a Kinect scanning the dancers' cloth vertically / MadMapper and video projection merging is used to seamlessly link the two projectors that cover the ulltra-wide screen
