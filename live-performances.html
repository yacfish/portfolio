Live Performances

Save the Last Trance for Me - 2018

Audio visual performance under the direction of Matthaei & Konsorten

Dedicated video player in MaxMSP-Jitter and Ableton LIVE featuring:
Editable cues for playback triggers & smooth transitions / Editable video effect chains using pixel shaders inside MaxForLive modules / Kinect tracking a lamp torch and mapping of its position to the screens to reveal or hide live video clips

Vimeo

UBIQUE - 2016

Ciné-Concert with the band Ethnophonics under the direction of Singkhéo Panya

Dedicated multi-channel video player in MaxMSP-Jitter and Ableton LIVE / Editable cues for playback triggers & smooth transitions / Editable video effect chains using pixel shaders inside MaxForLive modules / Fast video sampling in MaxForLive for playback from a percussive MIDI controller

Youtube

Infinite Journey/ In This Together… - 2015

Dance performance by Eveline Van Bauwel

Software developed in OpenFrameworks to create interactive color compositions and realtime effects that are the sole scenic element on stage / The colors pallets used are gathered in real time by a Kinect scanning the dancers' cloth vertically / MadMapper and video projection merging is used to seamlessly link the two projectors that cover the ulltra-wide screen

Blogpost

Les Yeux d'Argos - 2014

Online Dance Performance by Selma and Soufiane Ouissi

Software suite developed in MaxMSP-Jitter to allow two dancers to perform together from remote locations / Linux icecast server setup / Editable cues for playback triggers & smooth transitions / The result of the performance is only visible online

Youtube

The Death of Tarelkin - 2014

Dance Performance in collaboration with Alexandra Dementieva and students from MediaArtLab Moscow and Ballet School of Moscow

Software developed in MaxMSP-Jitter to control and mix video playback and live cameras on seven discrete screens with cues and smooth transitions

Vimeo

Perfectiøn - 2014

Dance performance by Bud Blumenthal

Real time white silhouette lighting following the performer on stage / Two Kinect and two video projectors facing down onto the stage are matched to cover the same surface / The Kinects volumetric image are processed to extract the silhouette of the dancer and erase the background / More matching is done in MadMapper to precisely match the silhouette with the dancer / Real time video effects controlled live through a midi controller in MaxMSP-Jitter

Vimeo

7adda - 2013

Theatrical performance by Jaouad Essounani

Software development in MaxMSP for live video on stage featuring: / Real time white silhouette lighting following the performer on stage using a Kinect / Real time video sampler to loop the silhouette with an accumulation option allowing the performer to create a crowd of silhouettes behind her / Cued playback & smooth transitions and composition of video sequences, realtime silhouettes and a realtime RGB image from a second camera / Cued DMX lighting controls

Youtube

Les Mystères de l'Ouest - 2012

"Ciné-Concert" directed by Singkheo Panya

Dedicated video player developed in MaxMSP-Jitter / Cued playback triggers & smooth transitions / Mapping module to conform to varying projection angles

Youtube

eVanescens - 2012

Multi-media performance by Todor Todoroff & Laura Colmenares Guerra

Software development in MaxMSP-Jitter / Cued playback triggers & smooth transitions / System for dynamic data stream routing / Mapping from audio analysis and MIDI controller to video playback triggers and effects parameters

Youtube

Fragments#43-44 - 2012

Multi-media performance by Gauthier Keyaerts in collaboration with François Zajega

Software development in MaxMSP / 25 channels audio player outputting a sound spatialization onto up to 8 speakers / A Kinect gathers the performer's position on stage and his gestures. / The position mapped onto a 5x5 grid is used to assign the gesture commands to audio effects and filters on the corresponding channels / A gesture also controls loading the next scene with to the next set of 25 soundfiles
