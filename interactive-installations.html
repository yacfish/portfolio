Oscillatory Motion - 2021

Interactive installation by Beatrijs Albers and Reggy Timmermans

Design of three robots' structures, drive trains and various other components using OpenSCAD / Structural parts machining on a CNC using Fusion360 at FabLAB iMAL / Drive train and other parts design and 3d printing / E-bike like lithium battery pack custom design using 18650 spot-welded and a BMS / Motor control and sensor data collection using two Arduinos on each robot / Sensors' data interpretation path planning and sound playback in MaxMSP runing on a miniPC on each robot

Lagunas - 2017

Interactive video installation by Laura Colmenaress Guerra

Parametric video sequencer in MaxMSP/ Jitter controlling three vertical displays / Arduino shield design with IR sensors to measure rotation of three levers for the users to interact with / Timeline-based UI designed in JS within MaxMSP to edit complex sequences / Dynamic assignment of users' input to control Video Players & Pixel Shaders / Interactive Scenario with multiple developments and endings

Chove Chuva - 2016

Technical direction for this video/aquatic installation by Cildo Meireles

Arduino actuated electro-vans to create pseudo rain in an enclosed environment / Creation of structure with different types of water dispensers to hang from the ceiling. / Dedicated Video Player programmed in OpenFrameworks to display clips of fake rain filmed in a studio are projected on the 4 walls of an open top cube / Kinect based presence tracking to link to the real rain in the space and the one video projected to the amount of the visitors in the cube

Moviola - 2015

Interactive multi-media sculpture by Marcio Ambrosio

Custom interactive 3d engine handling animation and trajectories of 3d unique objects or swarms of objects - developed in MaxMSP-Jitter / Design of an Arduino shield design to connect to IR sensors to mesure rotation of nine levers disposed on a sphere in the center of the space / The screen (a custom made half sphere designed by Marcio) is projected upon thru a round mirror and a precise mapping is obtained via MadMapper / They are actuated by the visitors to control the installation in different ways depending on the scene we're in / 12 interactive scenes were created by Marcio for the project inside of the engine
